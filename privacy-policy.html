Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Cari2Ge ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect through our website, why we collect it, and how it is handled.

1. What we collect

When you submit the application form on our website, we collect:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number and country
  • Education level
  • Gender
  • Program of interest

2. Why we collect it

We use this information solely to contact you about your application and to provide guidance on studying in Georgia (university selection, visa assistance, accommodation, and arrival support). We do not use it for unrelated marketing without your separate consent.

3. Legal basis

We process your data based on your explicit consent, given by checking the consent box before submitting the form.

4. Where your data is stored

Form submissions are processed and stored by Netlify, Inc., our hosting and forms provider. Netlify may store data on servers located outside your country of residence. Access to submissions is restricted to Cari2Ge staff who need it to respond to your application.

5. How long we keep it

We retain your data for as long as necessary to process your application and provide follow-up support, and no longer than 24 months after your last contact with us, unless a longer period is required by law.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at caribbean2ge@gmail.com.

7. Cookies

This website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. Only cookies strictly necessary for the site to function (if any) may be set.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how your data is handled, contact us at caribbean2ge@gmail.com or +995 511-73-49-73.
