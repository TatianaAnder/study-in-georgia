Services

At Cari2Ge, we provide full guidance and support for students pursuing higher education in Georgia. From choosing the right university to settling comfortably in Tbilisi, our goal is to make your transition smooth, stress-free, and successful.

University Application Assistance

We help you choose the best university that fits your academic goals and career path. Our team guides you through every step ofthe application process, ensuring that your documents meet each institution’s requirements.

Visa Application Support & Document Preparation

Our experienced advisors assist you in preparing and submitting your visa application, reviewing all necessary paperwork, andensuring compliance with Georgian immigration requirements.

University Interview Preparation

We provide personalised interview coaching to help you confidently communicate your strengths and motivation ensuring you makethe best impression during your university interview.

Airport Pick-Up

Arrive stress-free! Our team welcomes you at Tbilisi International Airport and ensures safe transportation to your accommodation.

Accommodation Sourcing

We help you find affordable and comfortable housing options near your university.

Residence Permit Assistance

Our team assists with the residence permit process, including document submission, translation, and application tracking to ensure a smooth experience.

Bank Account & SIM Card Registration

We help you open a local bank account and register a SIM card, allowing you to easily manage your finances and stay connectedfrom day one.

Local Orientation & City Introduction

We guide you through local markets (including halal-friendly options), transportation routes, and key student-friendly spots helping you settle into daily life in Georgia quickly and comfortably.

Our dedicated student support line is available to assist you with any concerns during your stay from university matters to personal well-being. You’re never alone in Georgia with Cari2Ge.
