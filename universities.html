About Tbilisi Medical Academy

Founded in 1992 by the distinguished Georgian physician Petre Shotadze, the Tbilisi Medical Academy (TMA) is one of Georgia’s oldest and most respected private medical universities. TMA has built a strong reputation for providing internationally oriented, high-quality education in Medicine, combining solid theoretical foundations with extensive clinical practice in leading Georgian hospitals.

The university offers fully English-taught programs that prepare students to meet global healthcare standards. Through modern simulation labs, clinical skill centers, and hospital affiliations, students gain practical experience from the early years of study.

TMA’s teaching faculty includes experienced Georgian and international doctors, ensuring that students benefit from both academic excellence and real-world medical insight. The Academy is committed to producing competent, compassionate, and globally minded medical professionals.

Student Life at TMA

Tbilisi Medical Academy offers a close-knit, multicultural community that welcomes students from around the world. Located in the heart of Tbilisi, it provides an ideal environment for academic growth and personal development.

TMA features a modern library equipped with both printed and electronic scientific resources, reading spaces, and learning zones for individual study. Other key facilities include an OSCE Center, scientific research and hematology laboratories, a VR teaching room, anatomical theatre, computerized examination center, and CCTV-secured computer labs.

Why Choose Tbilisi Medical Academy?

  • Established Legacy: One of Georgia’s first private medical universities with over 30 years of excellence.
  • Affordable Tuition: Competitive annual fees with flexible payment options for international students.
  • Fully English-Taught Program: The entire MD curriculum is delivered in English.
  • Global Recognition: Listed in WDOMS and recognized by FAIMER, Erasmus+, and the WHO.
  • Clinical Experience: Early exposure to real hospital environments through partnerships with Tbilisi’s top clinics.
  • Supportive Environment: Personalized academic support and mentorship programs.
  • International Community: A diverse student body that fosters cultural exchange and collaboration.
