About New Vision University

New Vision University (NVU), based in Tbilisi, Georgia, has quickly built a reputation as one of the most accessible and modern destinations for studying medical degrees. NVU offers a student-centered curriculum aligned with international medical standards, emphasizing practical training, early clinical exposure, and a straightforward admission process. Its modern facilities — including simulation labs and clinical partnerships — help students gain hands-on experience from the early stages of their studies. For many students, NVU has become an affordable and reliable pathway into medical education.

Student Life at NVU

NVU has a sizable and expanding student body, especially in its dental and medical programs. As more international students enroll at NVU each year, an inviting and comfortable international environment is created. Through clubs, events, and societies, the institution encourages active student participation while providing social and academic assistance. Through activities like cultural evenings, volunteer work, and group study sessions, NVU offers a dynamic, encouraging environment where students may develop both emotionally and professionally while studying overseas.

Why Choose New Vision University?

  • Globally Recognised Curriculum: NVU’s medical and dental programmes meet international academic and clinical standards.
  • Practical-Based Learning: Students gain early exposure to real clinical environments.
  • Affordable Tuition Fees: Among the most cost-effective options in Europe for international students.
  • English-Medium Instruction: Entire programme taught in English — ideal for international applicants.
  • Modern Facilities: State-of-the-art laboratories and simulation centres enhance hands-on learning.
  • Diverse International Community: With students coming from the UK, Caribbean, Africa and other corners of the globe, NVU is home to a diverse population of students.
  • Accreditation & Recognition: Officially listed by the WHO and recognised by major international medical organisations.

About the University of Georgia

The University of Georgia (UG), located in the vibrant capital city of Tbilisi, is one of the country’s top-ranked higher education institutions, known for its excellence in medicine and dentistry. Founded with a vision to deliver internationally recognised education, UG combines a modern academic curriculum with a strong focus on practical training and clinical exposure. UG’s facilities include state-of-the-art laboratories, simulation centres, and clinical training units, providing students with hands-on experience essential for developing professional competence. The faculty comprises highly qualified lecturers and practitioners, many with international experience, ensuring that students receive an education that meets global medical and dental standards.

Student Life at UG

Life at the University of Georgia is active, diverse, and supportive — offering students both academic excellence and a rich cultural experience. Tbilisi itself is a dynamic, student-friendly city where learners can easily connect with peers, join study groups, or explore Georgian culture. Beyond the classroom, UG encourages students to engage in social, cultural, and volunteering activities, helping them develop leadership skills and friendships that last well beyond graduation.Whether it’s enjoying campus events, networking with fellow international students, or exploring Tbilisi’s lively atmosphere, student life at UG blends personal growth with professional preparation.

Why Choose the University of Georgia?

  • Top-Ranked University: UG is one of Georgia’s leading institutions for Medicine and Dentistry, with internationally recognised programs.
  • Affordable Tuition Fees: Competitive pricing makes UG a cost-effective option for international students.
  • English-Medium Education: All medical and dental courses are taught entirely in English.
  • Modern Infrastructure: Equipped with advanced medical labs, simulation centres, and research facilities.
  • Global Community: A growing number of international students create a supportive and multicultural environment.
  • Accreditation & Recognition: UG is recognised by global medical bodies, including the World Health Organization (WHO) and FAIMER.
