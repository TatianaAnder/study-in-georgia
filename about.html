About Us

At Cari2Ge, our mission is simple; to make studying abroad easier, safer, and more achievable for students from the Caribbean and around the world. Founded by an international medical student who experienced the journey firsthand, Cari2Ge was built on genuine understanding, trust, and a passion for helping others succeed. We are more than just an agency, we are a community and a support system that guides students from the moment they consider studying abroad to the day they settle into life in Georgia.

Our Mission

To empower ambitious students by providing transparent guidance, personalized support, and reliable connections with top universities in Georgia. We aim to bridge the gap between students and institutions, helping you achieve your academic goals while feeling supported every step of the way.

Our Promise

At Cari2Ge, your success is our priority. Whether it’s helping you find the right university, navigating life in Tbilisi, or supporting you during your studies, we’re with you.
