Medicine

Why Study Medicine in Georgia?

Georgia has become one of Europe’s fastest-growing destinations for international medical students, offering affordable, English-taught programs that meet international standards.

Affordable Tuition Fees

Type of Program Average Annual Tuition (USD) Duration
Medicine $5,500 – $8,000 6 years

Structure of the MD Program

Phase Duration Focus
Pre-Clinical Years (1–2) 2 years Basic sciences: Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, etc.
Clinical Years (3–5) 3 years Rotations in Internal Medicine, Surgery, Pediatrics, etc.
Internship / Final Year (6) 1 year Hospital practice and research or thesis defense.

Global Recognition & Accreditation

  • World Health Organization (WHO)
  • FAIMER
  • ECFMG (USA)
  • GMC (UK)
  • Medical Council of Canada (MCC)

Top Medical Universities in Georgia

  • Alte University
  • New Vision University (NVU)
  • University of Georgia (UG)

Key Benefits

  • Affordable tuition & living costs
  • English-medium programs
  • Internationally recognised degrees
  • Safe, student-friendly environment
  • Hands-on hospital training
